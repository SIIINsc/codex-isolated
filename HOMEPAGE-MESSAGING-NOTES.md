# SpaceCombat.gg homepage messaging notes

## Core homepage job
Within a few seconds, the homepage should communicate:
- this site is about competitive Star Citizen PvP
- it is doctrine-first, not just raw data
- it offers practical value: training, interpreted meta, recommendations, and trusted insight
- it is curated by people who actually understand competitive play

## Strong homepage pillars
### 1. Doctrine first
What matters, why it matters, and how serious players actually think.

### 2. Interpreted meta
Not just ship/component stats, but what is viable, what is bait, and in what context a choice makes sense.

### 3. Practical learning
Training modules, communication doctrine, execution patterns, common mistakes, and improvement pathways.

### 4. Trusted org discovery
A future recommended-organizations surface that helps players find serious groups, training environments, and cultural fit.

## Trust signals to work into the homepage over time
- curated/editorial framing
- visible explanation of how recommendations work
- clear separation of sponsored and editorial placements
- concise proof of competitive credibility
- update rhythm / operator briefings / what changed recently

## Messaging guardrails
Avoid sounding like:
- a generic blog
- a stat database
- a fandom wiki
- a fake esports brand with nothing behind it

Aim for:
- concise authority
- practical usefulness
- premium tactical clarity
- earned confidence
